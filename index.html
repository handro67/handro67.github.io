It’s time to start thinking about Lacrosse!!!!

We're excited to announce that we will once again have sports training experts and elite lacrosse coaches at this year’s Winter Skills sessions! The program will also remain at 8 sessions from mid-January to mid-March (we will be off during FEB vacation). Sessions will be held at the New England Sports Center (formerly Derry Sports Zone).
