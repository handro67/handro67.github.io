Lacrosse Equipment

All players of each team will need to provide their own equipment. Goalie Equipment is provided for each team

Windham Youth Lacrosse provides team jerseys at the beginning of the season, and may be collected after the last game.

Boys Equipment

  • Sticks
  • Helmet
  • Gloves
  • Shoulder pads
  • Arm Pads
  • Mouth guard
  • Protective cup (see Shock Doctor)
  • Rib pads (recommended)
  • Cleats (recommended)

Girls Equipment

  • Girls Stick (has no pocket)
  • Goggles / Eyewear
  • Mouth guard
  • Cleats (recommended)

For information on retailers that carry lacrosse equipment for Boys and Girls Lacrosse, please see the Links section of the Windham Youth Lacrosse website
