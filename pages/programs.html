Programs

Spring Lacrosse Programs

Spring is the primary Lacrosse season, and Windham Lacrosse is a member participant in the New Hampshire Youth Lacrosse Association (NHYLA, www.NHYLA.com). Practices usually begin indoors at one of several indoor sports centers (contracted time) or in school gyms in April before the fields are dry enough to permit use. When the fields become available, practices move outdoors for the remainder of the season.

NHYLA organizes a 7 to 8 game schedule for each team with games played on Sundays. Practices usually occur during the week.

The following teams will be supported for the Spring season:
- Little Laxers Co-ed Instructional Program
- 8U Boys and Girls
- 10U Boys and Girls
- 12U Boys and Girls
- 14U Boys and Girls
There are coaching opportunities at all levels; no experience is needed; we will pay to get you certified.

The Little Laxers program will introduce kids to lacrosse. The focus will be on fun and exercise. The program includes a few indoor sessions in mid-March. Other than a lacrosse stick, NO equipment is needed.

Fall and Winter Lacrosse

Fall and winter Lacrosse is available on a smaller scale for those players who want to play during the traditional off-season. For boys, there is a weekly "skills development" practice one night per week. In addition, during the past several years, one or more youth level teams have been entered in either Granite State Lacrosse or Tomahawks Lax winter leagues run at area indoor facilities. Games are typically on Sundays and the level of play is generally above the "beginner" level. Speak to the Boys or Girls Directors to learn more about fall and winter Lacrosse.

High-School Level Lacrosse

Interested parents of high school aged players and Windham Lacrosse have also organized indoor league teams for high school age players the past several years. The focus here is skills and playing experience to build the Windham High School program. Contact the Boys or Girls program directors for additional information.

Other Lacrossse Programs

Tomahawks

Tomahawks Lacrosse program is among the top clubs in New England. Teams range from youth to elite lacrosse teams instructing at a high school level. Team brackets are as follows:

  • Youth Tomahawk Teams - pre middle school ages
  • 8th Grade Team - middle school ages
  • High School Tomahawk Teams - high school agess
  • Tomahawks Elite Lacrosse Teams - late high school ages - must tryout

More information about Tomahawk teams can be found at NHTomahawks.com

Granite State Lacrosse

Granite State Lacrosse offers multiple teams to play during the winter season indoors. Leagues include U-11, U-13, and U-15. Granite State Lacrosse also offers an Elite Team with exclusive tryouts at a high school level. The elite team plays at a high school level during the summer and competes in many tournaments as well.

More information about Boy's Granite State Lacrosse teams can be found at GraniteStateLacrosee.com

Middlesex Bears Lacrosse

Because of the current global situation, Middlesex Bears only offers small group skills for those that may not be as comfortable playng on a full team against full teams. MXB Lacrosse small group skills offers a more personal instruction and serves multiple age groups maxing out at 10 players per age group. More information can be found on their website at MXBGirlsLacrosse.com
