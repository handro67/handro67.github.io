Coach Certification

Head Coaches - Certification Instructions

All Windham Youth Lacrosse Head Coaches must be Level 1 Certified by US Lacrosse. Below are the steps to obtain the US Lacrosse Level 1 certification.

Please note that all costs will be reimbursed by Windham Youth Lacrosse. Please keep all documentation and receipts from each step.

Steps for US Lacrosse Level 1 Coaching Certification for ALL HEAD COACHES:

The general rquirements are:

  • Become a US Lacrosse Member
  • Complete an on-field training clinic
  • Complete two (2) on-line training courses
  • Complete a background check

Below are the steps required.

Step 1: Become a US Lacrosse Member here.

  • US Lacrosse Membership must be renewed yearly.

Reimbursement Process

Windham Youth Lacrosse will reimburse coaches for the following:

  • US Lacrosse Membership fee
  • US Lacrosse Level 1 on-line course fee
  • US Lacrosse Level 1 on-field instructional clinic fee
  • Positive Coaching Alliance (PCA) on-line course fee
  • US Lacrosse Level 1 certication application and background check fees
